Browser physics toy

Bouncy Balls

A small browser-based physics toy. Balls bounce inside a rotating circular wall until they find the moving gap and escape.

0/20 live balls

Keyboard extras: Space spawns, P pauses, R resets, A toggles auto-spawn, and D toggles the boundary.

Rotating Gap

The wall is a circle with a rotating angular gap.

Vector Reflection

Collisions bounce by reflecting velocity around the wall normal.

Escape Test

Escape compares the ball boundary angle with the current gap angle.

Canvas Port

The simulation runs directly in the browser with web-native Canvas.
